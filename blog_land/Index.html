When it comes to spending time with friends, who says you have to do things the ordinary way? Here at The Adventure Challenge, we believe in thinking outside of the box and getting creative with your friends. That’s why we’ve created this list of 11 unique and fun hangout ideas for you to try. If you’re ready to read and discover some pretty adventurous ideas to do with friends, then let’s dive in!.

#1 - Rent a Houseboat for a Weekend

Always dreamed of spending a few days out on the water, just you and your friends? This is your chance! Experience the ocean like never have before and rent a houseboat for the weekend. You’ll get to live on the water and dock near land whenever you want to, so you get the best of both worlds. If you’re looking for the perfect mini getaway, this hangout idea is sure to impress!
